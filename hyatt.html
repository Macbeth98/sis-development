Finest Hotel in Hyderabad - Banjara Hills

Park Hyatt Hyderabad is one of the most luxurious 5 star hotels in Hyderabad. With opulent architecture and unparalleled service, it’s not a stay but a journey that unfolds into rare and intimate experiences. From stunning Art in the hotel, to sumptuous Suites, a lavish Spa, Gourmet Restaurants and more, your experience would call for celebrations.

What Makes Your Stay Unparallelled:
  • The Residence: It's a home away from home, 41 fully furnished, ready to move in apartments & the best address in town for a luxurious stay.
  • Stylishly designed 185 rooms & suites where comfort blends with impeccable service
  • Central Location: Located in the upmarket Banjara Hills, access to all major corporates and city's sightseeing locations.
  • The Spa: Promises the ultimate in natural rejuvenation treatments.
  • Gourmet Restaurants: A dining experience not just sinful but a culinary drama. Specialty Italian restaurant; Tre Forni, The Dining Room including a huge buffet spread and The Living Room with a touch of local flavours.
  • Meet & Greet: Total space over 17000 sq ft designed for both business and social events.
